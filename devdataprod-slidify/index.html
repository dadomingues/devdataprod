The Project

This is my reproducible pitch presentation, where I describe what I did in my simple app project.

This project just works with "mtcars" dataset and makes the linear regression of two variables which the user choose the predictor and the the outcome is always the "mpg".

Take this amazing experience going to http://dadomingues.shinyapps.io/devdataprod-cp.
